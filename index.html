About amiss

Hello! My name is Avalon. :) Yes, Avalon. -not Avon, Avalin, or Avalawn (yes, I've gotten those before, too). Is it like "Avalon the car?" Yes, but I'm not a car. :)

I was raised in the "small" city of Los Angeles, California where it's sunny all year round and the beaches are spectacular. There are beautiful places to hike and explore God's creation.

Currently, I am a senior at Bob Jones University, and I hope to graduate in Biblical Counseling. After finishing my degree, I hope to pursue a Master of Arts in Biblical Counseling as well. I have a passion for others and seeing them understand the Gospel and apply it to their life.

I enjoy learning about various cultures, and one way I enjoy learning about cultures is through food! I love trying foods from all around the world. One of my favorite types of food is Asian cuisine. I love the different spices they use. Here you can find a recipe that I enjoy the most! Hibachi Steak, Rice, and Vegetables

Besides enjoying food and getting to know individuals, I enjoying going hiking and spending my time outdoors. Stars and clouds are just a couple things that I love the most about God's creation.
